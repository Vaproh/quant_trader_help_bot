# 📊 Strategies Overview

## Breakout
- Trades price breaking resistance/support
- Needs volume confirmation

## Pullback
- Trades retracement in trend
- Uses EMA / support zones

## Range
- Trades support/resistance bounces
- Works in sideways markets

## Momentum
- Trades strong directional moves
- Uses volume + trend strength

## Fake Breakout
- Trades traps (false breakouts)

## Pump
- Trades sudden volume spikes

## Overnight
- Time-based strategy (low liquidity moves)

## Volume Spike
- Detects abnormal volume activity
